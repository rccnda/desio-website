Privacy Policy for Desio Food

Effective Date: June 20, 2025
Last updated: July 16, 2025

Who we are

Desio Food (“we,” “us,” “our”) operates https://www.desiofood.com (the “Website”) located at Herenstraat 82, 9711 LL Groningen, Netherlands.

Data we collect

  • Personal Data provided by you: Name, email address, phone number (if provided via contact forms, reservations, or newsletter).
  • Order-related info: Delivery address, payment transactions (processed via secure third parties).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time stamps—collected automatically.
  • Cookies and trackers: Technical and analytics cookies for improving site functionality and performance. We don’t use profiling cookies.

How we use your data

  • To manage your reservations and respond to inquiries.
  • To process online orders and payments securely.
  • To improve our Website through anonymized analytics.
  • To communicate via email or phone (e.g. booking confirmations, updates).

Legal basis for processing

  • Performance of contract: e.g. fulfilling orders or reservations.
  • Consent: opt-in for newsletters or promotions.
  • Legitimate interests: ensuring site functionality, analytics, and fraud prevention.

Data sharing

We may share data with:

  • Service providers: such as reservation systems, payment processors (e.g. Mollie), email platforms (e.g. SendGrid, Gmail) under data‑processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities: if required by law.

International transfers

Data processed by our EU-based providers. If any data is transferred outside the EU (e.g. US-based email or payment services), transfers occur under GDPR-approved safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

Data retention

  • Reservation/order data: for 3 years (tax and record-keeping).
  • Newsletter data: until you unsubscribe.
  • Usage data: up to 2 years, unless anonymized earlier.

Cookies

  • Technical cookies: to enable website functionality (e.g. session).
  • Analytics cookies: to understand traffic and improve experience.
    You can configure your browser to reject cookies—but essential functionality may break.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have:

  • The right to access, correct, delete, or restrict your personal data.
  • The right to object to processing (e.g. marketing emails).
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time for marketing.
    To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@desiofood.com or write to Herenstraat 82, 9711 LL Groningen.

If dissatisfied, you may lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures—like SSL encryption and secure data storage—to protect your data.

Children

We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated—when material changes occur, we’ll update the Effective Date above, and promote the changes on our Website.

Questions?

Contact our Data Protection Officer at info@desiofood.com.
