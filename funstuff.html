Homemade Basil Pesto

Easy ‚Ä¢ 15 minutes ‚Ä¢ Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Toast pine nuts in a dry pan until golden (2-3 minutes)
  2. Add basil, garlic, and pine nuts to food processor
  3. Pulse until roughly chopped
  4. Add Parmesan cheese and pulse again
  5. Slowly add olive oil while processing
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Mix with cooked pasta and enjoy!

Chef's Tips

"Use only fresh basil and good quality olive oil for the best results. This pesto is perfect with our fresh pasta!"
